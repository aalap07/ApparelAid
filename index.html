ApparelAid™

Providing the less fortunate with the clothes they need.

Our Mission

International Care

We are currently based in the United States. Our goal is to reach out to as many impoverished areas around the world as possible, to help create the largest impact we can.

Quality

We will only send articles of clothing that are suitable for wearing. Each donation is checked by a member of our team to ensure that the receiving children are benefitting from the clothes.

Aid for All

All donations received and sent to the areas of need will be distributed equally among all who require them. This is done on a recurring basis.

Our Team

We are a group of high school students located in the USA. After personally witnessing some of the worst conditions in the world and the struggles that the children had to face there, we were deeply inspired to help in any way we possibly could.

Story


Our Goal


Over my school vacation in the spring of 2016, my family and I took a trip to Istanbul, Turkey to experience the great culture the country offers. During my trip, I witnessed hundreds of homeless and poor Syrian refugees that were forced to leave their homes. Children were begging for money, and families were separated because of such horrible conditions. It was devastating to see all the people in need of support and I wanted to find a way to directly help. Experiencing this made me realize how fortunate most of us are for being able to live in a country like America. Most of the people in need were once living in a house with their families and going to school just like most American children. Once the war started, they were forced to leave their own country to find a new home. Children who once went to school now have to grow up with no education and instead sell items like bracelets and tissues to provide for their families.

Visiting the Middle East inspired me to reach out to people in need. Until a solution is brought up to this crisis, it is our job as humans to stick up for each other in tough times like these. Nobody deserves to live in such dehumanizing conditions, so that's why I decided to start ApparelAid. The basis of this organization consists of collecting gently used clothes for families and children all over the world. With the help of many people, my goal for this organization is to make a positive impact on the world, no matter how small or large it may be.

-Maher Hammoud
